our mission
Technology is increasingly becoming more integrated into our everyday lives. At Exception Null, we believe that being able to write good code should be a skill everyone should have a chance to develop. That is why we built our platform.
When you subscribe to our service, you not only unlock features that allow you to build on your skills through challenges and collaboration that simulates industry, but you will also be providing an opportunity for someone else less fortunate to learn to code. Helping engineers improve is great. Allowing everyone the opportunity the opportunity to shine is even better. We encounter people with brilliant ideas everyday and we want to help them achieve their dreams in search of that better tomorrow for all.
